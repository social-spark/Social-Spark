Use Case:

Tiffany Brewer

Tiffany Brewer is eating lunch between classes and scrolling through FaceBook when she sees one of her friend post a picture that has a link to a website called "PromptMe." Her curiosity is peaked, so she clicks the link, and it takes her to the home page where she is greeted with a "login" option and a "create profile" option. She decides to create a profile and post a Prompt.

Interaction Flow

Precondition

Tiffany files out the necessary information such as name, email, username, date of birth and password to create her account.

Post condition

Tiffany wants to get some interactions on her post.

  • Tiffany clicks "create account"
  • Site validates information and directs them to the "create profile" page that provides her with the option to add a profile picture, add a "bio," and to select a minimum of 1 interest from a list of interests provided by "PromptMe"
  • Tiffany is directed to the "create profile" page where she picks a profile picture from her camera roll, adds her pronouns, her college and a little excerpt about herself in her bio and selects her interest them clicks submit.
  • PromptMe logs this information and directs Tiffany to her feed where they are then notified of a welcome prompt
  • Tiffany clicks to her notifications icon
  • Site produces a drop-down menu showing no notifications and one welcome prompt
  • Tiffany clicks the welcome prompt
  • site directs her to the new post page where and provides the prompt with the option of responding to the prompt with a picture, text or both.
  • Tiffany adds a picture to the welcome prompt post as well as a short message attached to the post and clicks post
  • Site publishes her post to her personal page as well as her feed
  • Site than takes her back to her feed where she can see her post as well as the nav bar and the trending prompts
  • Tiffany sees a prompt trending about food so clicks the prompt
  • Site opens a feed of everyone who has responded to that prompt as well as a button gives the option to respond
  • Tiffany looks at a few posts and likes some she thought were good
  • Tiffany decides to respond to the prompt, so she clicks the button to respond
  • Site directs Tiffany to a new post page and provides the prompt she chose to respond to
  • She snaps a picture of her lunch and adds a short excerpt about the prompt then clicks post
  • Site then publishes her post on her personal feed as well as the feed relating to food
  • Tiffany closes the page and returns to class
  • After class, Tiffany returns to "PromptMe" where she is still logged in
  • "PromptMe" shows she has a notification
  • Tiffany clicks the notification icon
  • Site provides a drop-down menu and in the dropped down menu it says she got 7 likes on her post about her lunch
