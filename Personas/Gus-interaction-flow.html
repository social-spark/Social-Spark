Interaction Flow

Gus enjoys looking into sports news and posting about the teams he is passionate about.

Precondition

Gus is logged into his PromptMe account.

Post-condition

Gus makes a post about sports and shares the link onto his FaceBook

  1. Site opens a page showing the prompt of the day, that is about sports, as well as the option to respond to the prompt by uploading a photo and/or writing an excerpt
  2. Gus decides to take a photo from his cell phone camera of him wearing favorite teams jersey and clicks post
  3. site posts the photo to his personal page as well as the newsfeed page
  4. Gus likes the post and decides to share the post on his facebook, so he clicks the share icon
  5. PromptMe opens a menu with a link of his post
  6. Gus copies the link provided by prompt me and pastes it to a facebook and posts it there
