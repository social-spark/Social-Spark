Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be a social media site that takes out the frustration of determining what to post.

Audience

This sites audience is meant to appeal to the people who use social media the most

Goal

The goal of this site is to create a place where people can post and not have to think to hard about what to post because that is provided.

Persona

Tiffany Brewer

Age: 19

Profession

Tiffany Brewer is a full time college student and works as a Starbucks barista three days out of the week. She takes prides in staying caught up with the latest trends and being among the first to participate in them. She is very active on social media and has a large presence on various websites and app.

Technology

Tiffany owns an iPhone 15 and a Mac Laptop, and she uses both to surf the internet, post and edit pictures and for school work.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Tiffany enjoys being active on various social media apps and websites. She posts multiple times a day on multiple different social media sites and her posts get lots of interactions like comments and likes. If she hears about a new platform that she finds interesting she will sign up and give it a week of use to see if she enjoys it.

Frustrations and Needs

Although Tiffany likes being active on social media she finds it difficult to think of ideas on what to post. She enjoys posting pictures of herself and activities she does but she also sometimes just enjoys posting a status or a paragraph of opinions of different topics.

Goals

Tiffany's goal is to be able to post about topics she interested. She likes being the first to use the newest sites and likes to be active on them and be the one who popularizes them. She likes posting content about her various interests but sometimes has trouble thinking of what to post.

User Story

As an active social media user, I need a site that can provide me with an idea about what to post based on my various interest.
