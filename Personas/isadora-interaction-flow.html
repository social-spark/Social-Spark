Use Case

User Isadora is a casual user of social media. She would like to be more active but finds it difficult to find topics that interest her to talk about. Her friend has suggested a social media website called PromptMe.net. Isadora created a new profile and has already posted her first prompt. She is interested in viewing the trending items and what others have posted.


Precondition: Isadora is logged in to her PromptMe.net profile.

Postcondition: She is interested in viewing what others have posted in the "trending" feed. It looks like there are 3 categories, "UNMHockey", "Sandia" and "Foodies".


Interaction-Flow

  • Isadora clicks on the "Foodies" in the Trending section of the left nav of her screen
  • PromptMe opens a page in the same window taking her to the trending "Foodies" page she selected
  • Isadora scrolls down the newsfeed
  • PromptMe displays posts and images that users have shared
  • Isadora likes a few posts
  • PromptMe displays the heart icon on the posts Isadora has liked
  • Isadora decides to share a few posts to her Instagram and Facebook accounts
  • PromptMe shares the posts to her Instagram and Facebook accounts and adds a box around the post with a link back to PromptMe.net
